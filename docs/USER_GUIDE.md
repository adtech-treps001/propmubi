## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit propmubi.com or open the mobile app
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone number
   - Password
4. Verify your email and phone via OTP
5. Complete KYC (upload Aadhar + PAN for full access)

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Saved Properties**: Properties you've bookmarked
- **Recent Searches**: Quick access to past searches
- **Alerts**: Notifications about price changes, new listings
- **Reports**: Your due diligence and analysis reports

## Rental Module

### For Tenants

#### Score-Based Deposit

**Standard problem:**
- Landlords ask for 6-10 months deposit
- Blocks ‚Çπ2-3L of your savings

**Propmubi solution:**
- Connect your CIBIL score
- If 750+: Pay only 1 month deposit
- If 650-750: Pay 2 months
- If <650: Standard 6 months

**How to use:**
1. Go to Rental ‚Üí Calculate Deposit
2. Enter monthly rent
3. Authorize CIBIL check (one-time)
4. See your deposit requirement
5. Save 5 months worth of rent!

#### Move-In/Move-Out Inspection

**Problem:**
- Disputes about "who damaged what"
- Landlords cut deposit unfairly

**Solution: AI-Powered Condition Report**

**Move-In Day:**
1. Open Propmubi app
2. Click "Start Move-In Report"
3. Take photos of each room:
   - Bedroom
   - Bathroom
   - Kitchen
   - Living room
   - Balcony
4. AI detects existing issues:
   - Cracks
   - Stains
   - Dampness
   - Paint peeling
5. Report is stored on blockchain (tamper-proof)

**Move-Out Day:**
1. Click "Start Move-Out Report"
2. Take same photos again
3. AI compares before/after
4. Shows only NEW damage
5. Auto-calculates deduction
6. Both parties sign digitally

**Result:** Zero disputes, 100% deposit refund if flat is maintained

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is Propmubi free?**
A: Basic features are free. Premium features:
- Due diligence reports: ‚Çπ99 each (first 3 free)
- Token of interest: ‚Çπ5,000 (refundable)
- Satellite monitoring: ‚Çπ499/mo
- Auction premium: ‚Çπ999/mo

**Q: How do you make money?**
A:
- Verification fees
- Token system
- Subscriptions
- Service commissions (legal, loans, interiors)
- Success fees (JV matchmaking)

**Q: Do you work with brokers?**
A: We aggregate listings from all sources, including brokers. But we also enable owner-direct listings to save brokerage.

### Buy/Sell

**Q: How accurate is due diligence?**
A: We aggregate from official sources (RERA, Govt). Accuracy >95%. But we always recommend physical verification for final decision.

**Q: What if due diligence shows red flags?**
A: We show you exactly what's wrong. You can:
- Negotiate price down
- Request seller to clear issues
- Walk away (no obligation)

**Q: Is token payment safe?**
A: Yes. Payment via Razorpay (RBI-licensed). Money held in escrow. 100% refundable as per policy.

### Rental

**Q: Will checking CIBIL reduce my score?**
A: No. We use "soft inquiry" which doesn't affect score.

**Q: What if landlord doesn't accept low deposit?**
A: Landlords are pre-informed about our score system. If they don't accept, we'll find you another property.

**Q: Is move-in report legally binding?**
A: Yes. Both parties sign digitally. Report is admissible in consumer court.

### Commercial

**Q: How accurate is footfall data?**
A: Based on actual delivery orders (Swiggy/Zomato). Accuracy ~85%. But always do physical site visit.

**Q: Can I get refund if report is inaccurate?**
A: If data is proven wrong, we refund 100% + ‚Çπ5,000 compensation.

### Auctions

**Q: Can I definitely buy at auction?**
A: No. Auctions are competitive. We only provide information and assistance. You need to bid yourself.

**Q: Are auction properties safe?**
A: Most are. But some have occupancy issues (tenants refusing to leave). Our due diligence flags this.

### Lifecycle

**Q: How often do you check satellite images?**
A: Weekly for paid subscribers. Monthly for free users.

**Q: What if someone encroached?**
A: We alert you immediately. You can:
- File police complaint (we provide evidence)
- Hire lawyer (we partner with LegalKart)
- Get local caretaker (we have partners)

### Community

**Q: Does my entire society need to join?**
A: No. Even 1 tower can start. Benefits increase as more join.

**Q: Is voting anonymous?**
A: No. Each vote is linked to unit number for accountability. But individual votes are private (only aggregate results shown).
